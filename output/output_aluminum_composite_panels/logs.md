+### 1.1 Definition & Scope
+Aluminum Composite Panels (ACPs), also known as sandwich panels, are a modern cladding material used extensively in construction. They consist of two thin, coil-coated aluminum sheets bonded to a non-aluminum core. The core is typically made from Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) for standard panels or a fire-retardant mineral-filled material for high-safety applications. ACPs are valued for being lightweight, durable, aesthetically versatile, and easy to install.
+
+### 1.2 Category Classification
+ACPs fall under the broader category of Building Materials, specifically within Cladding & Facades. They are a popular choice for both new construction and renovation projects.
+
+### 1.3 Market Context
+The primary market for ACPs in India is the construction and architecture industry. Key stakeholders include architects, builders, contractors, fabricators, and interior designers. These panels are procured for a wide range of projects, from large-scale commercial high-rises, airports, and malls to residential facades, retail shopfronts, and corporate signage. The Indian ACP market is a fast-growing segment of the global market, driven by infrastructure development and the demand for modern building aesthetics.

+### 2.1 Main Product Types
+*   **Standard ACP (LDPE Core):** Panels with a Low-Density Polyethylene core. These are suitable for low-rise applications, signage, and interiors where stringent fire regulations are not a primary concern.
+*   **Fire-Retardant (FR) ACP (Mineral Core):** Panels with a specialized core containing mineral fillers (like magnesium hydroxide) that inhibit the spread of fire. These are essential for high-rise buildings and public infrastructure to comply with fire safety norms. Common FR grades include:

+*   **Specialty Panels:**
+    *   **Aluminum Honeycomb Panels:** Extremely lightweight yet strong panels with a honeycomb aluminum core, offering superior flatness and rigidity.
+    *   **High-Pressure Laminate (HPL) Panels:** Often sold by ACP manufacturers, these are durable panels for exterior and interior use.
